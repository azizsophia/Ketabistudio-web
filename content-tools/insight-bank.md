## SOLID — safe to use
1. **ر ح م — raḥim (womb) & raḥma (mercy)** share the root; Ar-Raḥmān/Raḥīm derive from it. Backed by hadith: Allah "created the womb and derived its name from My Name" (Tirmidhi 1907, ṣaḥīḥ). ← carousel 01
2. **ق ل ب — qalb (heart) = "the thing that turns"** (root also gives taqallub, to flip/fluctuate). Du'a "Yā Muqallib al-qulūb, thabbit qalbī ʿalā dīnik" (Tirmidhi 2140, ḥasan). ← carousel 02
3. **ج ن ن — "concealed":** jannah (garden/Paradise), janīn (fetus), jinn, majnūn, junna (shield) all share the hidden-root. Strong "wait, really?" carousel.
4. **أ م ن — īmān (faith) = amn (safety):** to believe is to find security; pairs w/ Qur'an 13:28.
5. **ص د ق — ṣadaqah (charity) from ṣidq (truthfulness):** giving is the proof faith is真. "wa-ṣ-ṣadaqatu burhān" (Muslim 223).
6. **د ن و — dunya = "the lower/nearer one"** (superlative of adnā): the name warns not to overvalue it.
7. **و ق ي — taqwa = a shield** (wiqāyah); awareness of Allah as armor, not fear.
8. **ع ب د — ʿibādah / road worn smooth by walking** (ṭarīq muʿabbad): worship gets easier the more you walk it.
9. **ب ر ك — barakah = a camel kneeling and staying:** good that settles and keeps increasing (Lane's).
10. **ش ك ر — shukr:** a shakūr animal thrives visibly on little feed; gratitude turns a little into abundance. Qur'an 14:7.
11. **س ك ن — sakīnah (divine calm) shares root with maskan (home):** peace that "moves in" mid-storm; always "sent down" (9:40, 48:4).
12. **و د د — Al-Wadūd:** love that is *enacted/visible* (wudd, not ḥubb). Yaqeen source.
13. **ل ط ف — Al-Laṭīf:** gentleness + knowledge of the hidden; His gentleness *because* He sees the hidden weight you carry. Qur'an 67:14.

Unverified leads (verify before use): Al-Fattāḥ; ر ز ق rizq/Razzāq; ذ ك ر dhikr.

## ⚠️ DO NOT POST (fails verification)
- **insān ← nisyān (forget):** FALSE as etymology (insān root is أ ن س, uns/companionship). Only usable framed as a *tafsir reflection of Ibn ʿAbbās*, never as "the word comes from forget."
- **ṣalāh = ṣilah/connection:** FALSE — different roots (ص ل و vs و ص ل). Beautiful idea, not a fact. Exclude.
- **Word-count "miracles"** (dunya/ākhira 115 each, etc.): debunked cherry-picking. Never post number-counting claims — fastest way to get fact-checked.
